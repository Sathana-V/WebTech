Welcome to ABC Bellevue

In the early 1990s i had the vision of bringing high quality Moghul cuisine to the Seattle metropolitan area.I've opened three Chutney's restaurants in Seattle. Since ABC has been in Bellevue it's catering operations have grown significantly with catering to both the corporate sector, private parties, and special events.With clients like Microsoft, Oracle, Boeing, Infosys, Wipro, Tata Consultancy, Talisma, InfoSpace, Real Networks,Siemens, & GE. Special events catering for weddings, birthdays, and many private parties has also grow. We recently tied up with Westin Hotel in Bellevue to provide catering services for special events. We cater to groups of all sizes; from 20 tomato hundreds of guests.
